Carbonara

Eating a carbonara is a great way to enjoy a delicious Italian meal without having to spend time preparing it. Carbonara is a creamy and flavorful dish that combines pasta, bacon, eggs, and cheese. The combination of flavors makes for a truly unique and delicious experience. Plus, it's a great way to get your protein and energy for the day. So what are you waiting for? Try a carbonara today!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb of spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 4 ounces of bacon, diced

Steps to cook

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti or other pasta to the boiling water and cook until al dente.
  3. In a large skillet, over medium heat, cook the bacon until crisp.
  4. Add the garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  5. Add the eggs and Parmesan cheese to the bacon and garlic and stir to combine.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the egg mixture and toss to combine.
  7. Add the parsley and season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  8. Serve immediately.
