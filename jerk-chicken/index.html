BURGER

Jerk chicken is a delicious and easy way to add a unique flavor to your dinner. The jerk marinade combines flavorful spices like allspice, nutmeg, garlic, and thyme to create a savory, spicy flavor that will have your taste buds dancing. Not only is jerk chicken tasty, but it's also packed with protein and can be cooked quickly and easily on the grill or in the oven. Plus, the leftovers make for great lunches and snacks. So why not give jerk chicken a try and tantalize your taste buds?

Ingredients

  • Allspice
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Lime juice
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground ginger
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps to cook

  1. Soak chicken in jerk marinade for at least 2 hours.

  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

  3. Arrange the chicken pieces on a baking sheet.

  4. Bake chicken in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.

  5. Turn the chicken pieces over and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Serve with your favorite sides.
